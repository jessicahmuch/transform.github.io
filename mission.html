Our Mission

The first engagement in crisis we take is the abcd rule. Accept change, believe it shall be well with you, change with change and drive your life plan.

The kids group photo with the team.

We aim to find a common ground with the families, from the parents to the children displaced or apart due to migration, jobs, illness, natural calamities or internal issues. To unite them under the principles that are displayed based on love, life, light, faith, joy, hope and peace channelled with truth, founded in Christ Jesus, despite the families being desolate.

By faith we practically, through deeds reach and share the needs of others by loving them, praying for them, consoling them and supporting them as part of the amani family..

Gradually praising, glorifying and giving wisdom, thanks, honor, power and strength to our God on a daily basis and teaching the children to do the same. Receiving salvation and its blessings and passing on the same good news. We aim to slowly teach the children to lead a life full of life and peace. By accepting everyone in the community without judgement or segregation.



Blessing the children both born or unborn in Christ to be generational blessings. We aim to address the root to family challenges in the 21st century by seeking and being the solution. As a community in Christ we value the first born nation, Israel.


We intent to Intentionally raise generational leaders within the family tree, who can learn and defend their faith in the risen Christ Jesus. To win non belivers through logic, history, philosophy and sciencebefore their second phase of life(14years).


Seeking the right knowledge, understanding the application of the same information in the seven pillars of the society we live in. To be the light of your calling in your spiritual gifts, heart desires, abilities and experiences. Our logo represents more than out mantra, it defines the seven pillars. Family, Celebration, Religion, Education, Government, Economy and Media. Every pillar has its lies but equipping and empowering members with truth and dominion on earth
